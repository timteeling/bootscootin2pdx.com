Scooting Along the Oregon Trail – Portland, OR

By Sarah on June 24, 2015

We did in fact make it to Stumptown, Bridge City, Beervana, Rose City, whatever nickname you prefer. While we didn’t take the true Oregon trail, Sarah did probably get dysentery and had a close call with a bison in Yellowstone. We also learned that a travel blog probably won’t be our main source of income anytime soon, but it was fun to dream! Oh, and thanks for stalking us, it was a great ego boost.

On our journey we accumulated many scents that will take months to remove from our car and a record-breaking amount of mosquito bites. If we had a dollar for every bug bite we wouldn’t need a travel blog or a real 9 to 5, and would be living the Portland dream. The stench and uncontrollable itching was a small price to pay for the unforgettable trip we had. Cue Green Day’s “Time of Your Life.”

Since pictures are worth a thousand words, here are about a gazillion words.

We are now adjusting very quickly to the west coast lifestyle and probably will go back to the shower-less lifestyle very soon.
